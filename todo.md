## Review

### What was built

A distinctive **editorial legal-trade publication** aesthetic — not the generic "Modern SaaS" template feel. Specifically:

- **Typography**: Fraunces (variable serif, italic optical sizing) for the display H1 and section pull-quotes; Geist sans for body; Geist Mono for rank numerals and small caps labels. A pairing chosen to feel credibly editorial — closer to a "best of" magazine list than a marketing landing page.
- **Color**: Warm cream paper background (`#FAF9F6`), near-black ink, single oxblood accent (`#5B1A1F`) used sparingly — for the #1 entry's soft fill, italic display flourishes, and link hover states.
- **Layout**: Centered max-width 768px column. Hairline rules (`#E2DFD6`) between sections. Subtle dotted-grid paper texture on the body background. Each ranked entry uses a `[120px 1fr]` grid with an oversized tabular monospace rank numeral on the left.
- **#1 highlight**: Top Law Firms gets a soft accent-tint background and an "★ Editor's Choice" pill, distinguishing it without breaking the visual rhythm.
- **SEO**: Full meta + OG + Twitter tags, canonical URL, JSON-LD `ItemList` schema with all 10 entries, sitemap auto-generated by `@astrojs/sitemap`, `robots.txt` pointing at the sitemap.
